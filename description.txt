Adds extra keybindings for:
	- Chest actions
	- Dashing
	- Cycling all inventory slots
	- Dynamic shortcuts to inventory slots
	- Equipment Change

Instruction for the last two kinds of keybinds:
	1. Open your inventory
	2. Hover over a slot
	3. Press the keybind to bind it to that inventory slot
	4. Press it outside of your inventory to activate it
